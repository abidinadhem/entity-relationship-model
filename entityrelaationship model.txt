Entities:

Gymnasium
Member
Session
Coach
Attributes:

Gymnasium: Name, Addresse, TelephoneNumber
Member: ID-member, LastName, FirstName, Address, DateOfBirth, Gender
Session: Type of Sport, Schedule, Maximum Capacity (20)
Coach: LastName, FirstName, Age, Specialty
Relationships:

A Gymnasium can have multiple Sessions
A Member can register for a Session at a Gymnasium
A Session can have multiple Members
A Session is led by one or two Coaches
A Coach can lead multiple Sessions
